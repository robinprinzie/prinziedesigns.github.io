Hey there!

For some people, it is hard to find out what they want to do in life. I, on the contrary, always knew what I wanted to do, although the way to get there was often not easy. From very early on I had to cope with dyslexia. I had to work harder than other children to get results. Dyslexia also made me realize what I was good at and what was not in the cards for me.

Before becoming a graphic designer, I graduated in human resources. Although I got a good insight into the positioning of companies in their field, my heart was not really in it. At the end of these studies, I got back to my passion: conceptual thinking and creative design.

Dyslexia made me look at typo differently. For me characters, words, and paragraphs are the ingredients to create designs. I designed my logo the way a dyslectic person would read it. It is my mission to bring my strengths into your company and see it grow just as I want to see myself grow as a graphic designer.

What do I offer?

Since I have a cross-medial background, I know all ins and outs for both webdesign and print. Therefore I am capable of aligning all content which is distributed through different media. Thanks to a technical education I am familiar with different PDF-formats and techincal aspects such as die lines, color management etc.

You looking for more then just a designer? Since I have a commercial/business background (HR), I believe this can add value my conceptual thinking, my designs as of course the business side of this agency (which can’t be underestimated).

Besides that I would literally cross boundaries for my clients (with a lot of pleasure and excitement) and I will work for their companies with as much passion as if it was my own company. Both as human as designer I always feel the endless need to learn, to improve and to evolve. Not only to please our surroundings or customers, but also to feel satisfied and happy about myself. This means I am passionate and I will never settle for things that are just good enough.

SERVICES: -Total concepts - logo design - corporate identity design - packaging design - web design - campaign designs
